Hi, I'm Chad, General Manager at a bakery & cafe.


I created this site to showcase my skills and experience, and highlight the person I am.

I have worked in hospitality all my life, starting as an aprentice baker at 17 in the family business. After traveling and working in other hospitality businesses, I returned to the family business and took the reigns as the bakery production manager for 10 years, before taking on the general management of the business.


Now with many years of experience, awards won, challenges met, and my sights set on new horizons, I am lookig forward to entering the next phase of my career.


My current role includes:


  • personnel management of over 30 staff
  • problem solving
  • strategic planning
  • general admin
  • product development and supervision
  • marketing
  • design and management of our website
  • management of IT systems including point of sale
  • electrical / mechanical mainenance

I learned front-end coding in 2022 after years of working on and managing the upkeep of the Kidd's Cakes & Bakery Wordpress site
